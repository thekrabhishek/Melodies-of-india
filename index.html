The "Melodies of India" project is an expedition that aims to capture the heart and spirit
of India, transcending its deep-rooted cultures into a visually captivating series of
photographs, each narrating a unique facet of our incredible nation. These photographs
will serve as visual testimonies, encapsulating the diversity, traditions & vibrant tapestry
of culture alongside the banks of river Ganga
